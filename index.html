Our Heritage in Delhi NCR

Since 1904, Arjanmal Attarchand has celebrated the art of fine glassware. Born in Lahore and now flourishing in Delhi, we craft bottles and jars that merge timeless techniques with contemporary design for brands across Delhi NCR, Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad.

Each piece is meticulously hand-finished and thoughtfully designed, ensuring every bottle and jar is both functional and a statement of elegance for fragrance, food, and pharmaceutical partners.
