Our Beginning

A Legacy Born in 1949

In the bustling streets of old Lahore, Master Haji Muhammad Yusuf established what would become one of Pakistan's most revered perfume houses. With nothing but a passion for fragrance and a deep understanding of traditional distillation techniques, he began crafting attars that would captivate generations.

Our founder's vision was simple yet profound: to preserve the ancient art of attar making while creating fragrances that tell the story of our rich cultural heritage. Each bottle carries within it the essence of Lahore's soul, distilled through decades of expertise and unwavering dedication to quality.

Today, as we celebrate over seven decades of excellence, we remain committed to the same principles that guided our founder – authenticity, craftsmanship, and an uncompromising pursuit of olfactory perfection.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

From humble beginnings to international recognition, our journey spans three generations of dedication to the art of perfumery.

1949

The Foundation

Master Haji Muhammad Yusuf establishes the first atelier in Lahore's old city, beginning with traditional rose and sandalwood attars.

1965

Expansion & Recognition

Opening of the flagship store in Liberty Market and recognition as one of Pakistan's premier perfume houses.

1978

Second Generation

Muhammad Yusuf's son, Master Ahmad Yusuf, joins the business, bringing innovative techniques while preserving traditional methods.

1995

International Reach

First international exports to the Middle East and Europe, sharing Pakistani perfumery heritage with the world.

2010

Modern Collections

Launch of contemporary eau de parfum collections alongside traditional attars, bridging heritage with modernity.

2020

Third Generation

Master Farid Ahmad joins as the third-generation perfumer, introducing sustainable practices and digital innovation.

2024

75th Anniversary

Celebrating 75 years of excellence with limited edition collections and the launch of our bespoke fragrance service.
