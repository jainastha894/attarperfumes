Our Legacy

Crafting Excellence

With a legacy that traces back to Lahore, Arjanmal Attar Chand stands today as a trusted name in glass bottle packaging solutions for the fragrance , pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries. Built on generations of expertise, integrity, and innovation, we specialize in supplying premium glass bottles for attars, perfumes, essential oils, and beauty products.

Founded 1904

A Legacy Born from Passion

Our operations combine heritage craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver packaging that is both functional and refined. From bespoke attar vials to high-end perfume bottles, we provide tailored glass packaging designed to elevate every brand’s presence and preserve the purity of every product.

Driven by quality, precision, and reliability, Arjanmal Attar Chand continues to serve leading perfumers, exporters, and manufacturers across India and beyond — offering end-to-end glass packaging solutions that reflect excellence in every detail.

Our Process

The Art of Perfection

Every bottle and jar is shaped through a careful process that balances traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, ensuring durability, elegance, and consistency that meet our highest standards of excellence.

Material Excellence

We source only the finest raw materials—high-grade glass, durable aluminum, and premium finishes—to ensure that every bottle and jar meets international standards of strength, clarity, and elegance.

Crafted Precision

Our skilled artisans and manufacturers, backed by generations of expertise, shape each bottle and jar with care. From perfume vials to aluminum containers, every piece is designed to balance beauty with functionality.

Quality Assurance

Every batch undergoes rigorous inspections and testing. Only bottles and jars that meet our exacting benchmarks of durability, safety, and finish carry the Arjan Mal Attar Chand seal of excellence.
