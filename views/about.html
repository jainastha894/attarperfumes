Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1947

The Foundation

Master Haji Abdul Rahman establishes Attar Lahore in the historic walled city, beginning with traditional rose and jasmine attars crafted using ancient distillation methods.

1965

Royal Recognition

Awarded the prestigious title of "Royal Perfumer" by the Nawab of Bahawalpur, cementing our reputation among Pakistan's elite and establishing international recognition.

1982

Second Generation

Master Rahman's son, Ustad Mahmood, takes over operations and introduces innovative blending techniques while preserving traditional methods, expanding our signature collection.

2001

Global Expansion

Opening of our flagship boutique in Liberty Market and launch of international shipping, bringing the essence of Lahore to fragrance connoisseurs worldwide.

2024

Digital Renaissance

Launching our digital platform while maintaining our artisanal heritage, making our exclusive fragrances accessible to a new generation of perfume enthusiasts.
